The forum is for in depth discussions with the wiki community.

For questions about the wiki or small errors, please use the Community portal discussion page.

  • Note that this forum is NOT for suggesting new ideas about the game. That belongs on the feedback site.
  • This forum is for community discussion about the wiki as a whole. Generally, wiki issue reports should go on the Admin noticeboard and discussions about individual pages should go on their respective talk page.
  • This forum is a place for in depth discussions on changing wiki policy, or large proposals that require the collaboration of multiple editors to implement and cover many pages.

If you want to get watchlist notifications about new forum topics, add the forum category to your watchlist. (watch)

  • To get notified about topics getting closed as well, add the active forum category to your watchlist instead. (watch)
