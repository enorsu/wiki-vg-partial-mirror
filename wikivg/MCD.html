Fire Trail is an enchantment that can be found within the various armor of Minecraft Dungeons.

Tiers[edit | edit source]

Tier Effect Cost
I x fire damage per second 1 Enchantment Point
II 2x fire damage per second 2 Enchantment Points
III 3x fire damage per second 3 Enchantment Points

Usage[edit | edit source]

Fire Trail gives the hero the ability to create a trail of fire behind them when they roll that damages mobs before extinguishing after four seconds. The length of the trail of fire is determined by how far the hero rolled. The amount of damage dealt by the fire increases with each tier.

Fire trial is a rolling trigger, which means it is activated by the light feather artifact.

The damage dealt by fire trail is increased by the Fire Focus enchantment.

Stacking[edit | edit source]

Fire Trail is not stackable, meaning it will only ever appear in a single enchantment slot. This includes the gilded, unique, and regular enchantment slots.

Mobs[edit | edit source]

Enchanted mobs with this enchantment will summon purple fire instead of the regular orange-red like the hero's.

Built-into[edit | edit source]

Fire trail tier I is built-into the following armor:

  • Ghost Kindler
